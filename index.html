Projects

Collect 'em All

Ugh another Pokemon project! Collect 'em All is a React based browser game utilizing Firebase as the backend. Pick up pokeballs and use them at the PokeMart to buy Pokemon. You can switch your Pokemon at any time as long as you own more than one. Chat with others on your journey to collect 'em all!

Pokemon TCG Collector

Take a trip down memory lane! This is a React website built based on my love for Pokemon. Here, you can browse through the vast database of Pokemon trading cards utilizing a REST API. Cards can be looked up by sets or through the search bar. Listed on the card details page you can find the current market price and where to buy it.

Beefy Boi's Diner

Welcome to our humble diner! Here on our website, you create an account or checkout as a guest. Feel free to browse through our expansive menu built through PostgreSQL. Got a special event going on and wanna come in? Make a reservation! Or just plain hungry? Get your food delivered!

Planner Getaway

With Planner Getaway, you can plan-er getaway! This website allows users to organize a list to help plan their next trip. You can search for a location you would like to visit and the area will render using Google Maps' API. Afterwards, you can search for any activity or point of interests and they will populate in a list and on the map. From the results list, you can finalize your decision and add it into your personal list.
